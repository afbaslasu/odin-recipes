Description:

This colorful vegetable stir-fry is packed with fresh veggies and a savory sauce. Serve it over steamed rice or noodles for a quick and healthy meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 bell peppers (red and green), sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch (optional, for thickening)
  • Sesame seeds and green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the sliced onion and bell peppers. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until slightly softened.
  3. Add the zucchini, broccoli, and snap peas. Continue stir-frying for another 3-4 minutes.
  4. Push the veggies to the side and add the minced garlic to the center of the wok. Cook for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  5. Mix the garlic with the veggies.
  6. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, and cornstarch (if using). Pour the sauce over the veggies and stir well.
  7. Cook for an additional 2 minutes until the sauce thickens and coats the vegetables.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with sesame seeds and green onions.
