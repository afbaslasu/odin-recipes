Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, beat the egg and mix in the buttermilk and melted butter.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients, stirring until just combined (some lumps are okay).
  4. Gently fold in the fresh blueberries.
  5. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Grease with a little butter.
  6. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake.
  7. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  8. Serve warm with maple syrup and extra blueberries.
